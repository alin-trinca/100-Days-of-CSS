FAQ

What is lorem ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is placeholder text used to help designers avoid wasting their time on pages where they are inserting custom content.

What language is lorem ipsum dolor?

It is Latin. It comes from the book by Cicero written in 45 BC. The name of this book is de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (means "The Extremes of Good and Evil")

What is the meaning of Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum means ‘pain itself’. It comes from Dolorem Ipsum.

Why is Lorem Ipsum so popular?

It is now an industry-standard dummy text for designers, publishers, etc. When you don't know what text will fill the space you designed, you can just paste a lorem ipsum passage there.
